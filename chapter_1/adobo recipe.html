Adobo recipe(mine)

Adobo is a dish originating in the Phillippines.
Some even think that it's our favorite dish, and i can't lie about that.
Anyhow, there are a lot of ways to cook adobo, since people have preferences for theirs.
but in this recipe, i will be teaching you how to make mine.

Ingredients

  • whole chicken/meat (cut into portions)
  • a teaspoon of salt
  • 1 large sliced onion (any cut works but i preffer slicing them in crescent shape)
  • 3-5 cloves of diced garlic (usually depends on how big the chicken/meat is)
  • half a cup of oyster sauce
  • half a cup of vinegar (you can use white vinegar if you want something mild)
  • half a cup of soy sauce
  • one teaspoon of black pepper
  • one teaspoon of garlic powder
  • half a cup of mountain dew, otherwise use 2 tablespoons of sugar(white or brown works)
  • one and a half cup of water
  • a tablespoon of honey

method (step by step)

  1. in a large bowl, add the chicken and rub it with salt
  2. take a large pot and heat it in medium heat and add oil. Once the oil is hot enough, add the sliced onion and the diced garlic in the
    pot and saute it until the garlic and onion softens and the aromatic odor starts to hit your nose
  3. once the sauted onion and garlic are done, you can then add the salted chicken/meat and keep on stirring so it mixes in with the flavor of the saute.
    Continue until the chicken/meat slightly gets cooked.
  4. once it does, add in the vinegar, oyster sauce, water, mountain dew/sugar, soy sauce, garlic powder, and black pepper and stir.
  5. stir everything in the pot, close the lid and let it simmer for about 25-30 minutes.
  6. after simmering, open the lid and give it a good stir again. after stirring, put the heat on the low and let it simmer again for a few more minutes. minimum of 5-10 minutes.
  7. while letting it simmer, you can now add the honey and stir it gently
  8. while stirring, taste the adobo first and adjust the ingredients if needed. And if you feel like its to your liking already, you can now turn off the stove and serve.
