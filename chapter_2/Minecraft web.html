Plot

In minecraft, theres no such thing as plot. You start with nothing but your fist, spawning in a random spot in a big and open world where sky and bedrock is the limit. You then break, craft and mine, letting the endless possibilities flood your creativity. In the world you created, you have freedom. Freedom to create anything you can imagine, things like building, fighting mobs and bosses, it only comes down to you.

What Made Minecraft

Minecraft was created by a man named Markus Persson in a small apartment in Stockholm. He released it in 2009, and slowly, quietly, people started playing. Soon it became a home for millions. Today it belongs to Mojang Studios, a team in Sweden who still care for it like a garden. You can play it on a computer, a console, a phone—almost anywhere. You can survive, create, or simply wander.

The Creator/Devs

Markus "Notch" Persson made the first version alone, sharing it with the world one small update at a time. Then Jens "Jeb" Bergensten took over, quietly shaping the game into what it is now—adding new flowers, new animals, and keeping the soul intact. Behind them is Mojang, a small group of people who treat Minecraft like a quiet, gentle thing that does not need to be rushed. They simply let it grow.
